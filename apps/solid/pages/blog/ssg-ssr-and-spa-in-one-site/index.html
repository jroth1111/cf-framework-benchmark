SSG, SSR, and SPA in one site

2025-04-02 • 7 min read

Modern sites are usually hybrid:

  • Some pages must be interactive (SPA-ish)
  • Some pages benefit from SSR (fast first paint + SEO)
  • Some pages are perfect for SSG (blog/docs)

This benchmark implements all three so you can test your real constraints.
