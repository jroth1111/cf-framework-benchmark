Why this benchmark exists

2025-01-08 • 4 min read

This repo is not trying to crown a universal "best" framework.

It gives you a repeatable way to compare:

  • Load performance (TTFB-ish, LCP, CLS)
  • Repeat views (cache + parsing + route transitions)
  • Client CPU + memory under identical interactions

The site is deliberately small but representative: listings, a chart, and a blog.
